How it works

Your digital legacy in 4 simple steps

1

Set up your account

  • Sign up for Beyond Demise
  • Choose between Farewell (free) or Disclosure (confidential)
  • Set your preferences on when to release your messages
2

Create your messages

  • Write your final goodbyes or important information
  • Decide who should receive each message
  • For Disclosure, we'll invite your recipients to set up their secure access
3

Secure your secrets

  • Farewell messages are safely stored on our servers
  • Disclosure messages use an extra layer of security that only your chosen recipients can unlock
  • All messages are protected with industry-leading encryption techniques
4

Automatic release

  • We regularly check on you using using your chosen method
  • If we don't hear from you, we initiate the release process
  • Your messages are sent to your chosen recipients when the time comes
