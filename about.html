The idea that changed everything

It all started with a near-death experience. Our founder, Sarah, was in a serious car accident that left her hospitalized for weeks. During her recovery, she couldn't stop thinking about what would have happened to her digital life if she hadn't survived. Who would have access to her accounts? How would her loved ones be notified? What about the unfinished letter to her estranged father? This brush with mortality sparked an idea: a secure way to manage our digital legacies and ensure our final wishes are respected, even after we're gone.
